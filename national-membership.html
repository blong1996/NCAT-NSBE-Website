National Membership

Join NSBE today! We want you to become a member of the most dynamic group of students, professionals, advisors, and friends who are working toward eliminating disparities in education and achievement in engineering. Becoming a national member is the easiest way to join our efforts in fulfilling the mission. Regardless of where you might fit on the engineering life cycle, NSBE has a place for you.

Membership Types

  • NSBE Jr. – Pre-college: Grades 6-12, only $5 per year!
  • Collegiate – Undergraduate and graduate students, only $15 per year!
  • Professional – Includes technical professional and graduate students, only $50 per year!
  • Lifetime – Make the ULTIMATE commitment to NSBE. Dare to SOAR in RARE AIR…
  • International - For individuals who reside outside of the U.S. and Canada. International membership rates apply for all categories except Lifetime Membership

Member Benefits

  • Access to an unparalleled network of diverse engineers and scientists
  • Access to over $500,00 worth of scholarships
  • Online Job Placement Center
  • Access to 7 Annual Career Fairs through Fall Regional Conferences and NSBE’s Annual Convention
  • Annual subscription to NSBE’s magazines
  • Discounted registration at NSBE Conventions
  • Product discounts
